How to Get Started

Follow these simple steps to start creating your medical question bank

1

Create Your First Question

Use our rich text editor to create questions with formatting, images, and multiple choice options. Add detailed explanations to help students learn.

2

Organize by Categories

Categorize questions by exam type (USMLE, COMLEX), subject (Cardiology, Neurology), and topics for easy organization and retrieval.

3

Use AI Generation (Optional)

Set up AI integration with your API key to generate questions from topics or content. Choose from OpenAI, Google Gemini, or DeepSeek models.

4

Import Existing Questions

Import questions from Excel or CSV files using our template format. Perfect for migrating existing question banks or bulk uploads.

5

Export and Share

Export your question bank as Excel files for sharing with colleagues or as PDF documents for printing and offline study.

6

Manage and Update

Easily edit, delete, or update questions as needed. All changes are automatically saved in your browser for future sessions.
